In-Person Registration

Learning on Graphs Conference, 2025

We are pleased to announce that registration for the Learning on Graphs Conference (LoG 2025) is now open.
The conference will take place at Arizona State University, Phoenix, AZ.

  • Click here to in-person register
  • Click here to online-only register

Registration Dates and Fees

Type Period Fee (USD) Notes
Early In-Person Registration Before Nov 10, 2025 $120 Includes full conference access and catering
In-Person Registration (with guaranteed catering & T-shirt) Nov 11 – Nov 25, 2025 $150 Catering and T-shirt size guaranteed if registered by Nov 25
In-Person Registration (without guaranteed catering) Nov 26 – Dec 2, 2025 $150 Catering not guaranteed; T-shirt based on availability
Online-only Registration Until Dec 9, 2025 Free Access to live online sessions only
To ensure smooth management and planning, we strongly recommend registering before November 10, 2025.
T-shirt and catering guarantees are only available for registrations completed by November 25, 2025.

The In-Person Registration Covers

  • Full access to all conference sessions
  • Three lunches and coffee breaks
  • One welcoming reception dinner (Dec 10)
  • LoG 2025 swag (e.g., T-shirt)

Important Dates

  • Hotel booking cut-off date: Nov 10, 2025
  • Early in-person registration deadline: Nov 10, 2025
  • Guaranteed catering & T-shirt selection cut-off: Nov 25, 2025
  • In-person registration deadline: Dec 9, 2025

Notes

  • In-person registration will be handled through the ASU Special Events platform.
  • A confirmation email will be sent upon completion.
  • For questions, please contact the organizing committee at logconference@googlegroups.com.
